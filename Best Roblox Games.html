Classic Roblox Games

1. Be Crushed By A Speeding Wall!

BCBASW is a fun game where there is a speeding wall, trying to crush you! The wall only comes when somebody presses the button, so teamwork is key! As you play, you get points for making it in safe zones and oof'ing. You will oof alot! You can buy stuff for points to help you beat the game!

2. SUPER BLOX 64!

SB64 is a mario-like game on Roblox! The graphics are really good, and it is hard. It does take a while to beat, but its not impossible. It's what makes it fun! There are also red coins which you can collect for a extra challange!

3. Hole in the Wall

HITW is a game where you (or your team) fit in shapes to earn your team points! At the end, the team with the most points win, and get coins! Most of the time, it is really hard to be the best in the server. Keep trying, and you will some day!

4. Sword Fights on the Heights IV

SFOTHIV is a game where you spawn in a map, and fight with lots of unique swords! You can find swords scattered around the map, neatly placed on little stands. Swords include; Firebrand, Ice Dagger, Illumina, Darkheart, Windforce and Venomshank. Also, if your lazy you can buy swords to always go in your invetory for 50-500 robux!
